Meet Sophie

My lifelong passion for animals, turned into a career.

Hi! My name is Sophie. I am a 19-year-old pet content creator from Bedfordshire, UK. Animals have always been my source of comfort and confidence, and that connection grew into my career.

I started my pet journey at the age of 9 by volunteering in a grooming salon, then built experience across dog kennels, dog walking, pet sitting and supporting rescued dogs. Today, I combine that knowledge with content creation to help pet brands connect with real pet owners.
